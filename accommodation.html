PDEx 2026: Venue & Travel Information

Formation, Evolution, and Characterization of Planetary Systems
September 20--22, 2026, KIAA, Peking University

Register

Workshop Venue

  • The workshop will be located at the KIAA (the Kavli Institute for Astronomy and Astrophysics) , at the north of the Yanyuan (main) campus of Peking University.
  • The KIAA building will be able to hold workshops for ~50 people.
  • The PKU Yanyuan campus is also a world-famous scenic spot with rich historical heritages.

Accommodation

There are many hotels near the campus; the closest ones to the venue are:

  • The Lakeview Hotel Beijing (北大博雅国际酒店)
    (~ 5 minutes walk from KIAA)
  • Zhongguan Xinyuan Global Village PKU Beijing (北京大学中关新园)
    (~ 20 minutes walk from KIAA)
